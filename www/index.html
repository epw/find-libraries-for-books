Find Libraries for Books

Warning: May be very slow for large numbers of books.

About

The source code for this site is at https://github.com/epw/find-libraries-for-books.

This site just barely does the minimum the author requires of it. There are a lot of improvements that will hopefully be added if the author has time, such as selection of which Overdrive and Hoopla library networks to query, and links to the particular book records when they are found.
